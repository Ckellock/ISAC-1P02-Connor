As I mentioned in the "About Me" section, I also like to write poetry. During my senior year of highschool, I took a course titled "Writers Craft," where I learned how to write poetry in several forms. Below is a sample of a free verse poem that I created for an assignment.

Every groove, every spin, every listen,
They never change, don’t they?
Every stream, every download, every purchase,
They all convey feelings.
Music is what makes me.
What makes me move,
What makes me love,
What makes me dance.
What makes me breathe.
If one does not have music in their life, then why be living at all?

You don’t need drugs to get there,
They’re hiding in plain sight.
Hundreds of thousands of stories that you can have
Here, right now, or
Tonight.
You don’t need a compass to find what you're looking for,
All you need is time.
Some music you won’t like, some you will, and that’s fine.
What is your prescription? What really makes you go?
What will get your body shaking, right to the dancefloor?
But of course, you don’t need to dance, no, not all the time.
Sometimes its just nice to sit and relax, headphones on,
Or in the car, speakers blaring,
even earbuds in;
I’m fine.
